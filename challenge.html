About

Mohamed Salah was born on June 15, 1992, in Nagri, Gharbia, Egypt. As a child, Salah was not very good at academics. Following this, Salah tried to find things that could cover up his shortcomings until eventually, Salah found his love for football. Having difficulties to develop his academics, Salah continued to sharpen his skills in playing football. His days were filled with watching football matches on television. At that time, he idolized some Arab footballers.
